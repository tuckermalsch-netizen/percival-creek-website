Cidery Process

Whole Fruit.
Real Cider.

We press whole Washington-grown fruit — nothing from concentrate, no artificial flavors, no corn syrup. Our students learn to work with raw ingredients and let fermentation do what it was meant to do.

🍎
Washington Fruit

Sourced from Washington orchards. Whole-fruit pressing for maximum flavor integrity.

❄️
Cold Fermentation

Letina tanks, cold fermentation, slow and controlled for clean, fruit-forward character.

🧪
QA Lab Testing

Every batch tested for pH, dissolved oxygen, and microbial stability before release.

Our Cidery

Built to
Produce.

Our on-site cidery features stainless Letina fermentation tanks, cold-press capability, and a full QA lab. Students learn to produce cider at commercial scale — the same equipment used by professional cideries across the Pacific Northwest.
