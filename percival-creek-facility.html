Our Facility

Professional Equipment.
Student Precision.

The Percival Creek production facility at Tumwater Craft District is a fully equipped, commercially licensed brewery, cidery, and distillery — purpose-built for both education and real-world production.

Every piece of equipment is state-of-the-art. Every process is overseen by professional instructors alongside SPSCC students who bring an uncommon level of care, curiosity, and commitment to every batch.

Percival Creek Brewing

The Brewhouse

Our 10-barrel brewhouse is a fully commercial, state-of-the-art system operated year-round. From grain-in to glass, every step is executed with precision — and with the enthusiasm of students who genuinely love what they do.

🌾
Mashing & Lautering
Full-grain mashing with precise temperature control across multiple rests. Automated lautering for consistent wort clarity and efficiency.
  • 10 BBL mash tun capacity
  • Multi-step temperature mashing
  • Fly sparging system
  • Precise wort collection
🔥
Boiling & Hopping
A 4-vessel brewhouse with a dedicated whirlpool vessel and an attached Hop Back for recirculation through the kettle or whirlpool.
  • 10 BBL steam kettle
  • Dedicated whirlpool vessel
  • Hop Back attached for recirculation
  • Recirculation through Kettle or Whirlpool
🧪
Fermentation & Conditioning
A full complement of fermenters, brite, and lager tanks with glycol temperature control, plus dedicated Letina tanks for spirits wash fermentation.
  • 2 × 10 BBL Fermenters
  • 2 × 10 BBL Brite Tanks
  • 2 × 10 BBL Lager Tanks
  • 2 × 17 BBL Letina Tanks (Wash Fermentation)
🥫
Packaging
In-house canning line with Counter Pressure Filler and kegging station. We package to order for draft, retail, and direct-to-consumer distribution.
  • Counter Pressure Filler
  • 12 oz & 16 oz can formats
  • 1/2 & 1/6 bbl kegging
  • Label application on-site
🍎
Cidery
Dedicated cider production using fresh Washington-grown fruit. Whole-fruit pressing, cold fermentation, and bright tank conditioning.
  • Whole-fruit pressing capability
  • Dedicated cold room
  • Fruit addition dosing system
  • Fining & filtration options
🔬
QA & Lab
On-site quality assurance lab with full sensory evaluation, microbiological testing, and analytical chemistry capabilities.
  • DO, CO₂ & pH testing
  • Microbiological analysis
  • Sensory evaluation panel
  • Full ABV & density testing
Percival Creek Distilling

The Distillery

Our TTB-licensed distillery operates a single integrated still — a copper pot still with an attached column — for a total still capacity of 250 US gallons per run. Every spirit we produce, and every spirit we contract-distill, is made with precision, patience, and the high standards of our program.

Contract Production

We Produce.
You Brand.

Percival Creek offers full-service contract brewing, cider making, and distilling for brands at every stage — from concept to shelf-ready product.


Whether you're launching a new craft brand, scaling an existing recipe, or need a reliable production partner in the Pacific Northwest, our facility and our team are ready to make your vision a reality.

How It Works

From First Call
to Finished Product

1

Initial Consultation

Tell us about your brand, your product vision, and your production goals. We'll assess fit, discuss capacity, and outline realistic timelines and pricing.

2

Recipe & Spec Sign-Off

We refine or develop your recipe, agree on raw materials, target profile, and packaging specs. Everything is documented before production begins.

3

Pilot Batch

We produce a pilot batch for your approval. You evaluate, provide feedback, and we dial in the final recipe before committing to a full production run.

4

Full Production & QA

Full-scale production under QA oversight. You receive batch records, analytical data, and a product that's ready to go to market under your label.
