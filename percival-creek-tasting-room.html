Come In.
Pull Up a Stool.
Stay a While.

Step into a space where every detail is as intentional as the spirits we pour. Our Tasting Room blends the inviting, cozy feel of a living room with the sophisticated atmosphere of a high-end music lounge. Whether you're settling in for a familiar favorite or hosting a private gathering, our top-notch customer service ensures an experience as distinctive as our drinks.

Explore a menu of truly unique beverages crafted specifically with our own products — like our signature Raspberry Stout, featuring the rich, student-brewed Percival Creek American Stout.

The Space

A Space as Crafted
as Our Drinks


The tasting room's design reflects the handcrafted nature of what's inside. Every material, every detail has been chosen to create an environment that feels as distinctive and intentional as the beverages we serve.


Whether you're sampling the latest seasonal release or settling in with a familiar favorite, you'll find good company and great drinks.
